Welcome to our Guest House

Guest house OLIMPIA is located in the center of the resort city Sveti/Saint Vlas, on one of the main streets that leads directly to the beach strip which is 500 meters away. The house consists of double and triple rooms . Additionally, there are two private apartments on the fourth and final floor, suitable for four people in each. The two apartments have enormous balconies with an incredible view of the sea and harbor. One of the apartments has a kitchen, furnished with a bar counter, stove, sink, dish ware and utensils. Furthermore, there are two other apartments for the accommodation of 6 individuals per apartment, as each includes three bedrooms, two bathrooms, a modernly equipped kitchen and laundry machine.

Sveti Vlas

The town is located at the foot of the southern slopes of the Balkan Mountains, in the northern part of Burgas Bay. A settlement was founded at the present place of Sveti Vlas in the 2nd century AD by Thracians and Greeks, then called Larissa.
